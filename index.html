~ ROAD TO LEGACY ~

Our Flagship event, Road To Legacy connects students from three universities with industry experts in IT careers. Explore paths from Software Engineering, AI and Cyber Security to freelancing and Teaching in IT. Engage in Q&A, networking, and gain real world insights. Get a glimpse of the future. Learn, Plan & build yourself accordingly.

The Perfect Event for a 1st year IT student

"More than an event. It's a transformative experience" Join, Experience and set the course for your future success.

Learn

Led by industry experts, there are sessions on career paths, responsibilities, salaries, and skills required in those jobs and insights to pass the interviews. Learn real world case studies, and interactive Q&A.

Connect

Establish valuable relationships within your community, fostering collaboration, mentorship, and opportunities for personal and professional growth.
